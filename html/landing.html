Features

Bad Habit Tracker

The Bad Habit Tracker is designed to assist users in identifying, monitoring, and overcoming undesirable habits. By logging occurrences and triggers of bad habits, users can gain insights into patterns and develop strategies to replace them with healthier behaviors. The tracker provides support through reminders, tips, and progress reports to help users achieve a positive lifestyle change.

Goal Tracker

The Goal Tracker feature helps users set, manage, and achieve their personal and professional goals. Users can define specific objectives, set deadlines, and break down their goals into manageable tasks. With progress tracking, reminders, and motivational insights, the Goal Tracker ensures users stay on track and celebrate their accomplishments along the way.

Meditation

The Meditation feature offers a variety of guided meditation sessions and mindfulness exercises to help users reduce stress, enhance focus, and improve overall mental well-being. Users can choose from different meditation styles and durations to suit their preferences and schedules. With soothing audio and step-by-step guidance, the Meditation feature promotes relaxation and inner peace.
